ABOUT
Technology is often perceived as invisible and untouchable, as something that connects everyone even miles away. PARASITE reveals the proliferation of connection, placing a geographic barrier to users who, only if within the same boundary, will be able to see their simultaneous presence through the metaphor of microorganisms.

The entire space of the website is constructed from the users' location; what the user sees of all other parasites outside its boundaries, is only an archive of the footprint of other activities.

This website is an academic project designed in Bovisa’s parasite - Italy, Milan - during the course of Creative Coding 2022/23 at Politecnico of Milan.
