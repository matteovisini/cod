MICROVERSE
ABOUT
Technology is often perceived as invisible and untouchable, as something that connects everyone even miles away. PARASITE reveals the proliferation of connection, placing a geographic barrier to users who, only if within the same boundary, will be able to see their simultaneous presence through the metaphor of microorganisms.

The entire space of the website is constructed from the users' locations; what the user sees of all other mother cells outside its boundaries, is only an archive of the footprint of other activities.

THE TEAM

PARASITE does not represent only the simulation of the behaviour of microorganisms. PARASITE is also an academic project designed during the Creative Coding Course in Politecnico di Milano.
