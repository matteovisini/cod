ABOUT
Technology is often perceived as invisible, as something slightly surreal that connects everyone even when miles away. It may be seen as what takes us closer to the metaphysical, the mystic or the abnormal.

We thought of a way to show a paradoxical opposite side of technology. Parasite is the first website which you can access with your GPS location, but where you can not see others’ locations unless they are in your same physical area.

You can leave your trace with the automatically generated cell in the space and see to which parasite this will belong to, but discover only your neighbours’ cells and explore other parasites with limited visibility.

THE TEAM

PARASITE does not represent only the simulation of the behaviour of microorganisms. PARASITE is also an academic project designed during the Creative Coding Course 2022/23 in Politecnico di Milano.
